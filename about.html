About Us

Backbone Distributions was founded by brothers Connor and Sean in 2017 when neither of them could find a reliable delivery service that ticked all the boxes. Growing up in Ottawa, Sean and Connor wanted to make sure that they serve their community first and continue to provide small-town service in the Big City today.
